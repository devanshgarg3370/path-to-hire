Why Your Resume Matters

Your resume is your first impression. Recruiters spend only a few seconds scanning resumes, so clarity, relevance, and ATS optimization are essential.

Resume Structure

Contact Information

Include your name, email, phone number, LinkedIn, and GitHub portfolio.

Professional Summary

A short introduction highlighting your strengths, goals, and experience.

Education

Mention your degree, university, CGPA, and graduation year.

Skills

Add programming languages, frameworks, databases, and tools relevant to the job.

Projects

Showcase practical projects with measurable outcomes and technologies used.

Certifications

Mention relevant certifications from trusted platforms and organizations.

ATS-Friendly Resume Tips

  • Use a clean and simple layout.
  • Avoid tables, graphics, and excessive colors.
  • Include keywords from the job description.
  • Use professional fonts.
  • Keep your resume one page (for freshers).
  • Save as PDF unless instructed otherwise.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long should a resume be?

For freshers, one page is usually sufficient.

Should I include hobbies?

Include them only if they are relevant or demonstrate useful skills.

Should I add a photo?

Unless specifically requested by the employer, avoid adding a photo.
