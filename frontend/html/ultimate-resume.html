Why Your Resume Matters

Your resume is your personal marketing document. Recruiters spend only a few seconds deciding whether to continue reading or move to the next applicant.

Essential Resume Sections

Contact Information

Include phone, email, LinkedIn and portfolio.

Professional Summary

A short introduction highlighting your strengths.

Education

Mention degree, university and CGPA.

Skills

Technical and soft skills relevant to the role.

Projects

Showcase real-world work with measurable impact.

Achievements

Certifications, awards and competitions.

ATS Resume Optimization

✅ Use standard headings
✅ Match job keywords
✅ Save as PDF
✅ Avoid tables & graphics
✅ Use action verbs
✅ Quantify achievements

Common Resume Mistakes

  • Using generic objectives.
  • Spelling and grammar errors.
  • Too many colors and graphics.
  • Irrelevant experience.
  • Missing keywords.
  • Poor formatting.
