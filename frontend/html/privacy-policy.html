Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Path to Hire collects, uses, protects, and manages your information while helping you prepare for placements, internships, and your career journey.

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Path to Hire. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. By using our platform, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

  • Full Name

  • Device & Browser Information
  • Cookies & Analytics Data

3. How We Use Your Information

4. Data Protection

We use secure technologies and industry-standard security practices to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

  • Secure Authentication
  • Protected Databases

Path to Hire uses cookies to keep you logged in, remember your preferences, improve website performance, and provide a better user experience.

6. Sharing Your Information

We never sell your personal information. Your information may only be shared when required by law, with trusted service providers, or when necessary to operate our services securely.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your personal information
  • Update your profile
  • Delete your account
  • Request data removal

  • Withdraw consent where applicable

8. Resume Uploads

Uploaded resumes are used only to generate AI insights, ATS analysis, skill gap reports, and personalized recommendations. They are never shared publicly without your permission.

9. Third-Party Services

Our platform may integrate with third-party APIs, analytics providers, authentication systems, or career services. These providers have their own privacy policies.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any major changes will be posted on this page, along with the updated revision date.
